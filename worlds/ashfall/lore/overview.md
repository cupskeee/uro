# Ashfall

The Sundering broke the old empire and left the Vael Coast to rot. Where once imperial galleys
harbored at Vel, now grey tides gnaw the piers and the Duchy of Vel rules a town it can barely
feed. The nobles keep their walls; the poor keep their heads down.

Beneath the water something old is stirring. The Saltborn — a cult grown fat on the drowned-god
faith — have begun taking people from the docks, one a season, and the Duke's men look the other
way. Some say the cult means to finish a ritual older than the empire itself, and pull Vel down
under the tide for good.

It is a grim, low-magic, political world: no heroes come to save Vel, only people deciding what
they are willing to do about the water rising.
